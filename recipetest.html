Aunt Anne's famous Crab Dip

This dip is one of my most requested potluck bring-alongs. It is delicious of course, but it is also incredibly easy to make.

The Ingredients:

  • Cream Cheese, room temperature
  • Can of Crab Meat, drained
  • 2 T chopped onion
  • 2 T horseradish, this is key!
  • Smoked Paprika

Here's what you do:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
  2. In a medium bowl combine cream cheese, crab meat, onion and horseradish.
  3. Scoop into an oven safe dish.
  4. Sprinkle with the paprika until the top is well dusted.
  5. Bake for 20-30 minutes. You will know it's done when it turns golden brown and bubbles around the edges.
  6. It will be very hot! Allow to cool for 5 minutes.
  7. Serve with your favourite crackers and/or sliced veggies. I love rounds of cucumber slices myself.
  8. Enjoy!
